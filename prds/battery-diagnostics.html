SigFeed

A personalized news intelligence platform that collects articles from selected sources, scores them for signal, and delivers more relevant information through automation.

Problem

News consumption becomes inefficient when high-value items are mixed with repetitive or low-signal content and there is no consistent filtering workflow.

Objective

Build a Python-based pipeline that gathers articles, interprets relevance, and automates recurring delivery so the information stream becomes more useful.

Core Requirements

  • Ingest online news and articles through APIs and web scraping
  • Use AI-assisted filtering and scoring to prioritize higher-signal content
  • Store results in a structured format for repeated access
  • Implement automated workflows for recurring collection and personalized delivery

Tools

  • Python
  • APIs and web scraping
  • Automation workflows
  • AI-assisted filtering

Success Criteria

  • Consistent recurring collection from selected sources
  • Clear prioritization of more relevant content
  • Reduced time spent sorting low-value articles manually
